Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition

A very simple introduction

What is Dungeons & Dragons?

Dungeons & Dragons (D&D) is a tabletop role-playing game where players create characters and tell a story together. One player, called the Dungeon Master (DM), controls the world and the challenges.

Characters & Classes

Each player controls a character with a class such as Fighter, Wizard, Rogue, or Cleric. Your class determines what abilities and powers your character has.

How the Game Works

Players describe what they want to do, and dice are rolled to determine success or failure. The most important die is the 20-sided die (d20).
